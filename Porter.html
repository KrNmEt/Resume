A Porter's Five Forces Analysis of Reliance Jio's Market Entry Strategy

Associated with University of Delhi (DU)

I took a deep dive into how Jio didn't just enter the Indian telecom market—they systematically dismantled the existing barriers to entry. Using Porter's Five Forces as a framework, my analysis deconstructs how they strategically addressed:

  • Threat of New Entrants Overcoming massive capital requirements and spectrum costs.
  • Intensity of Rivalry Triggering a data-led price war that reshaped the entire industry.
  • Bargaining Power of Buyers Shifting the value proposition entirely to the consumer with free voice and low-cost data.

This project was a fascinating look at audacious strategy and flawless execution. It’s a powerful case study for anyone interested in competitive strategy, market disruption, and business model innovation.
